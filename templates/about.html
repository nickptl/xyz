Key Statistics

  • Divisions: 4 (East, North, South, West)
  • Depots: 37
  • Bus Stations: Numerous across the city
  • Fleet Size: Over 3,900 buses

Infrastructure and Facilities

Depots and Workshops: 37 bus depots categorized into 4 regions. Each region is headed by a Regional Manager, and each depot is headed by a Depot Manager. Maintenance and repair facilities at various levels.

Tyre Retreading Plants: Facilities for tyre retreading to ensure safety and longevity of bus tyres.

Bus Body Building: Facilities for building and maintaining bus bodies.

Printing Press: For printing tickets, passes, and other necessary documents.

Information Technology and E-Governance

  • GPS/PIS Based Bus Tracking: First in the country to introduce GPS/PIS based Bus Tracking & Passenger Information System for monitoring buses.
  • Electronic Ticketing Machines: Over 14,000 electronic ticketing machines introduced in all schedules.
  • Online Services: Launched its own website for online time table, passenger reservation, and e-ticketing. Integrated Depot Management System implemented in all depots. Computerized Inventory Management System for central and divisional stores.
  • Public Entertainment System: Providing entertainment options for passengers.
  • BSNL OFC MPLS Connectivity: Ensuring robust communication infrastructure.
  • Computerized Pass Issue System: For daily and student passes.
  • Statistical Analysis: Carried out through computerized systems for better decision-making.
  • E-Tendering Process: Established for procurement and other processes.
  • Automatic Driver Testing System: Implementing systems for driving skill tests.
  • Online Recruitment: For staff across all categories.
  • Video Conference System: Implemented between the central office and all division offices.

Future Developments

Bus Management System (BMS): Introduction of a Bus Management System to collate real-time data for multiple purposes including monitoring driver behavior, route rationalization using GPS data, and more efficient charging of electric buses.

Dedicated Dashboard: A system to provide real-time information on bus schedules, availability, and operational data.

Contact Information

Customer Support: Available for passenger queries and support.

Emergency Contacts: Important contact numbers for emergencies related to bus services.

User Guides & FAQs

How-To Guides: Instructions on using the web app, booking tickets, and tracking buses.
